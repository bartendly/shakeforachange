WHAT IS SHAKE FOR A CHANGE?

Global Impact. Local Empowerment. Zero Greenwashing.

Shake for a Change puts the spotlight on those who never get it. Every event is a real opportunity: for local bartenders to headline their own night, share their culture and story, and connect with guests, brands, and mentors.

All profits from each night go directly to fund the next unseen bartender — in their city or a new country. No waste, no elitism: just inclusion, circularity, and the best cocktails you never knew existed.

Why support us?

  • Measurable social visibility: Track the number of bartenders revealed, events held, and real lives impacted.
  • 100% transparency: All profits are reinvested for future events and training, with public reporting.
  • Full Altos/Tahona alignment: Local empowerment, real sustainability (plastic-free, local suppliers, zero flight for guests), and content you can reuse for your own RSE/ESG communication.

HOW IT WORKS

  1. Discovery: Unseen bartenders are nominated or apply via our site. We focus on those with real talent but little access to the “big” stage.
  2. Takeover Night: The bartender hosts a pop-up in their city, shares their signature, and runs the show — always with support from our team.
  3. Amplify & Connect: Each bartender gets professional photos, video, and PR. We promote their story globally and help connect them with future gigs.
  4. Reinvest & Repeat: Every euro from the night funds the next unseen bartender, creating a self-fueling cycle of change.
